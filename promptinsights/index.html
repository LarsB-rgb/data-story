// The PromptInsights Platform

We Track the Full
Conversation Journey

Existing tools analyze a single synthetic prompt. We simulate real human multi-turn conversations across the entire customer journey — from first question to final decision.

Stage 01

Informational

The user seeks knowledge. They ask AI to explain categories, concepts, and options. Your brand either exists in this educational layer — or it doesn't.

Stage 02

Navigational

The user refines their search. They ask for leading players, comparisons, and trusted sources. Where does AI steer them?

Stage 03

Commercial

The user evaluates. Feature comparisons, pricing questions, and use-case fit — AI shapes what the user believes about your product vs. competitors.

Stage 04

Transactional

The user is ready to act. AI provides recommendations — and you either win that recommendation or you lose to a competitor who invested in their AI presence.

// What We Measure

Five Metrics That
Actually Matter

We track key metrics to set a baseline, benchmark performance, and continuously monitor success across AI platforms.

Metric Definition
🏆 WIN RATE
How often AI recommends your brand as the primary choice A brand can be mentioned frequently but still lose customers if positioned as secondary. Win Rate measures Recommendation Strength — not just visibility.
MENTION RATE
% of conversations where your brand appears at least once The foundational visibility metric — are you even in the room?
SHARE OF VOICE
Your brand's share of total mentions across all conversations How much of the AI narrative do you own, compared to the entire competitive landscape?
CONVERSION
Win Rate ÷ Mention Rate When AI mentions you, how often does that visibility actually convert to a recommendation? Quality of presence, not just quantity.
SENTIMENT
Language tone when AI discusses your brand Scored from −1 (negative) to +1 (positive). AI tone shapes purchasing intent — even if the user never reads a review.
